# Sarva AI - Product Documentation

## 1. Product Overview
**Sarva AI** is your "Universal Expert" companion. It leverages advanced Artificial Intelligence to provide accurate, context-aware advice across multiple domains including Law, Finance, Education, and IoT.

Designed for simplicity, Sarva AI allows you to interact naturally via text or voice, making expert knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of language or technical expertise.

## 2. Key Capabilities
- **Expert Agents**:
    - **LawBotix**: Legal advice and constitutional knowledge.
    - **ArthAI**: Financial planning, market trends, and investment advice.
    - **Miss Nova**: Interactive English language tutoring.
    - **IoT Expert**: Hardware and smart home automation guidance.
- **Smart Routing**: You don't need to choose an expert. Just ask your question, and Sarva AI finds the right agent.
- **Voice-First Experience**: Speak to Sarva just like a human, and hear the response back in natural speech.
- **Language Bridge**: Ask in English, get answers in Tamil, Hindi, Spanish, and more.

## 3. Getting Started Guide

### 3.1 Accessing the Application
1.  Ensure the application server is running (consult your technical administrator).
2.  Open your web browser and navigate to: `http://localhost:8080`

### 3.2 Using Chat
1.  **Type** your question in the input box at the bottom.
2.  Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button.
3.  The relevant agent (e.g., *LAW Agent*) will stream the answer instantly.
    - *Tip*: You can copy code snippets or formatted text using the "Copy" button.

### 3.3 Using Voice
1.  Click the **Microphone** icon.
2.  Speak your query clearly.
3.  Click the icon again to stop recording.
4.  Sarva AI will process your speech and respond with both text and audio.

### 3.4 Changing Language
- Use the **Language Dropdown** in the top-right corner to select your preferred output language (e.g., switch from *English* to *Tamil*).

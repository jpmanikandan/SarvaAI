YOGA POSES AND BENEFITS

1. Tadasana (Mountain Pose)
   - Benefits: Improves posture, strengthens thighs, knees, and ankles.
   - Instructions: Stand with bases of big toes touching, heels slightly apart. Lift and spread your toes.

2. Vrikshasana (Tree Pose)
   - Benefits: Improves balance and stability in the legs. strengthens the ligaments and tendon of the feet.
   - Instructions: Stand tall, shift weight to left foot, place right sole against left inner thigh.

3. Adho Mukha Svanasana (Downward-Facing Dog)
   - Benefits: Calms the brain and helps relieve stress and mild depression. Energizes the body.
   - Instructions: Come onto floor on hands and knees. Lift knees away from floor. Lengthen tailbone.

4. Trikonasana (Triangle Pose)
   - Benefits: Stretches and strengthens the thighs, knees, and ankles. Stretches hips, groins, hamstrings, and calves.
   - Instructions: Exhale and step your feet apart. Turn right foot out 90 degrees. Extend torso to right.

5. Savasana (Corpse Pose)
   - Benefits: Calms the brain and helps relieve stress and mild depression. Relaxes the body.
   - Instructions: Lie on back, heels spread, arms by side, palms facing up. Focus on breathing.

BREATHING TECHNIQUES (PRANAYAMA)

1. Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril Breathing)
   - Benefits: Lowers heart rate and reduces stress.
   - Instructions: Close right nostril with thumb, inhale left. Close left, exhale right. Inhale right, close right, exhale left.

2. Kapalabhati (Skull Shining Breath)
   - Benefits: Energizing, clears respiratory passages.
   - Instructions: Short, powerful exhales and passive inhales.

MEDITATION
- Mindfulness: Focus on your breath. When mind wanders, gently bring it back.
- Loving-Metta: Send good will to yourself, then friends, then neutral people, then difficult people, then entire universe.

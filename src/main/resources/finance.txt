
Finance and Market Analysis:
1. NIFTY 50 is a benchmark Indian stock market index that represents the weighted average of 50 of the largest Indian companies listed on the National Stock Exchange.
2. Sensex is the benchmark index of the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).
3. Compound interest is the interest on a loan or deposit calculated based on both the initial principal and the accumulated interest from previous periods.
4. Diversification is a risk management strategy that mixes a wide variety of investments within a portfolio.
5. Liquidity refers to the ease with which an asset can be converted into ready cash without affecting its market price.

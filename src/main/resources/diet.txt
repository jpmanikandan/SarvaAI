DIET AND NUTRITION GUIDELINES

MACRONUTRIENTS
- Protein: Essential for muscle repair and growth. Sources: Chicken, Fish, Eggs, Tofu, Lentils, Greek Yogurt.
- Carbohydrates: Primary energy source. Sources: Oats, Rice, Quinoa, Sweet Potatoes, Fruits, Vegetables.
- Fats: Hormonal health and energy. Sources: Avocados, Nuts, Seeds, Olive Oil, Fatty Fish.

GOALS
1. Weight Loss (Caloric Deficit)
   - Consume fewer calories than TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure).
   - Focus on high protein and vegetables to stay full.
   - Drink plenty of water.

2. Muscle Gain (Caloric Surplus)
   - Consume slightly more calories than TDEE.
   - Prioritize protein intake (1.6g to 2.2g per kg of bodyweight).
   - Compound lifts in gym + sufficient sleep.

SAMPLE MEAL PLAN (Balanced)
- Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and protein scoop / Omelette with spinach.
- Lunch: Grilled chicken breast, brown rice, broccoli.
- Snack: Greek yogurt or a handful of almonds.
- Dinner: Baked salmon or stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables.

HYDRATION
- Drink at least 3-4 liters of water daily.
- Electrolytes are important if sweating heavily (gym/sauna).

GENERAL TIPS
- Avoid processed sugars and seed oils where possible.
- Eat whole foods 80% of the time, enjoy treats 20% of the time.

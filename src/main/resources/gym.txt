GYM WORKOUTS AND EXERCISES

1. PUSH WORKOUT (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
   - Bench Press: 3 sets x 8-12 reps
   - Overhead Press: 3 sets x 8-12 reps
   - Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
   - Lateral Raises: 3 sets x 15 reps
   - Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets x 12-15 reps

2. PULL WORKOUT (Back, Biceps, Rear Delts)
   - Deadlift: 3 sets x 5-8 reps
   - Pull-Ups / Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets x 8-12 reps
   - Barbell Row: 3 sets x 8-12 reps
   - Face Pulls: 3 sets x 15 reps
   - Bicep Curls: 3 sets x 12-15 reps

3. LEGS WORKOUT (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves)
   - Squat: 3 sets x 6-10 reps
   - Romanian Deadlift: 3 sets x 8-12 reps
   - Leg Press: 3 sets x 10-15 reps
   - Leg Curls: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
   - Calf Raises: 4 sets x 15-20 reps

PRINCIPLES
- Progressive Overload: Gradually increase weight, frequency, or number of repetitions in your strength training routine.
- Form: Prioritize correct form over heavy weights to prevent injury.
- Rest: Allow muscles 48 hours to recover before training the same group again.

CARDIO
- HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training): Short bursts of intense exercise alternated with low-intensity recovery periods.
- LISS (Low Intensity Steady State): Walking, cycling, or swimming at a steady pace for 30-60 mins.
